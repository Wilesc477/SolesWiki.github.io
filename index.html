Soles

# Description The northern continent where the campaign takes place. Much of the continent consists of grasslands and cold deserts. The main kingdoms that rule of the northern continent include [ExcitedLand!!](locations/ExcitedLand!!), [Kingdom of Rezg](locations/kingdom%of%rezg), [Khar Theocracy](locations/Khar%Theocracy), [Onzonian Empire](locations/Onzonian%Empire), and the [Kingdom of Grorju](locations/Kingdom%of%Grorju). See maps with borders and Biomes Below. ![borders map](./Locations/Soles(borders).png) ![biomes map](./Locations/Soles(Biomes).png) The Kingdoms aside from ExcitedLand!! have had repeated wars over the past couple centuries (see [80 year war](events/80%year%war), [war of 1000](events/war%of%1000), [Red year](events/Red%year)), but are in a period of uneasy peace for the moment. The players begin their quest in west Soles, in the town of [Wehret](locations/Wehret).
